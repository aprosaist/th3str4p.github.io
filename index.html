Hi, I'm Ben!

You could use a lot of words to describe me - Christian, husband, coffee addict - but in my opinion the best word to use would be student. In all aspects of my life, I've never stopped learning.

I do my best to live by the so-called Golden Rule: "You must love your neighbor as yourself."

A wise man once said, "But when I reflected on all the works that my own hands had done and on all the hard work that I had toiled to accomplish, I saw that everything was futile, a chasing after the wind; there was nothing of real value under the sun." In other words, there's more to life than work. I wholeheartedly believe that, which is why I try to offer lasting value to my family, friends and colleagues.
