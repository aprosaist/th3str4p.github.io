Hi, I'm Ben!

In other words, there's more to life than work. I firmly believe that, which is why I try to offer lasting value to my family, friends and colleagues.

I offer web design, graphic design and digital marketing services. Some clients prefer to work with me on an ongoing basis, while others call me in when they need a little extra help. Either way, you'll find that working with me is different - in a good way.
