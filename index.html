Hi, I'm Ben!

I'm a professional IT consultant and web developer based in sunny Phoenix, AZ. I enjoy helping small businesses establish their web presence and solve internal IT problems. I own and operate Techie Support, LLC.

Professional IT Consulting

Small businesses have special needs when it comes to their IT. They often don't have large budgets to spend on technology like bigger companies. I enjoy helping them implement the best possible solutions within their budgets. I also enjoy helping them solve problems with their existing infrastructure in the most cost effective way possible.

Learn more

Professional Web Design

I love building responsive and featureful websites for small businesses. I can build simple "static" landing sites to provide a fast, responsive and cheap web presence to small businesses and microbusinesses with extremely tight budgets. I can also build full-featured sites backed by the industry leading CMS WordPress for small businesses that need advanced features like appointment booking and e-commerce.

Learn more

Professional Private Cloud

Small businesses rely on the cloud more today than ever before. I can build and deploy custom branded private cloud technologies to provide things like private cloud storage and help desk ticketing systems for small businesses that prefer the privacy, flexibility and cost efficiency of their own private cloud.
